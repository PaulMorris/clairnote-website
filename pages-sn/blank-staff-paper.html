Print the following PDF files to write music by hand in Clairnote SN. The first file has standard two-octave Clairnote SN staves, the second has three-octave staves, and the third has four-octave "grand staves" for piano. You can also download the LilyPond files that were used to generate each PDF file. They can be used to produce blank staff paper with larger or smaller staves and different numbers of staves per page. (The LilyPond files require the clairnote.ly file. See Software: LilyPond.)
